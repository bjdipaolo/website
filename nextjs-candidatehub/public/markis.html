How Markis works

Markis recommends. Your team decides. Always.

There's a lot of noise about AI making decisions in hiring. Markis doesn't work that way — and that's intentional. Every recommendation Markis makes is transparent, grounded in real activity data, and requires a human to act on it. Your team stays in control of every hire, every outreach, and every decision. Markis just makes sure you never miss the right moment to act.

Transparent
Every recommendation shows its reasoning. No black box decisions.
Advisory
Markis suggests. Your team approves. Every action requires a human.
Explainable
Recommendations are grounded in real signals and activity data — not guesswork.
Strategic intelligence

Ask Markis anything about talent.

Markis acts as your on-demand talent intelligence strategist. Review a job description, audit your hiring process, build a nurture campaign, or analyze retention risks — just by asking. No configuration required. No waiting for a report.

  • Review and score job descriptions instantly
  • Identify bottlenecks and drop-off risks in your funnel
  • Build nurture campaigns for any role or talent pool
Requisition intelligence

Know what's working before you post.

Before a role goes live, Markis reviews it and gives you a Readiness Score — flagging missing compensation data, location gaps, and content weaknesses that reduce applicant quality. It doesn't just tell you there's a problem. It tells you exactly what to fix and why.

  • Readiness scoring on every requisition
  • Specific warnings with recommended fixes
  • Quick actions to launch campaigns directly from the review
Journey architecture

Multi-step journeys built in seconds.

Describe your campaign objective and audience, and Markis designs a complete multi-step engagement journey — including email themes, wait periods, and conversion strategy. You get a full campaign blueprint ready to review and launch. Edit anything, approve everything.

  • Complete journey blueprints from a single prompt
  • Email themes, pacing, and CTA strategy included
  • Build workflow only, or generate full messaging — your choice
